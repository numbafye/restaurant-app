About

"Burgiza" is a popular restaurant that serves delicious burgers and pizzas made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. With a welcoming atmosphere and friendly staff, it's the perfect spot for a casual meal with friends and family, and also offers takeout and delivery options.
